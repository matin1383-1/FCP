Welcome to the course page for Foundations of Computer and Programming.
This course introduces students to the fundamental concepts of computing, algorithmic thinking, and the basics of programming.

Whether you're studding mathematics, computer science, or related fields, this course is designed to help you with the essential tools and mindset needed to approach problem-solving through code.
You'll learn not just how to program, but how to think like a computer scientist.
